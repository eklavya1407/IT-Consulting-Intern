## 3. Role & Responsibilities

### Role: IT Consultant Intern

As an IT Consultant Intern, my responsibilities included:

#### 3.1 Client Requirement Gathering & Analysis
- Attended internal meetings to understand ongoing client projects
- Assisted in preparing structured requirement documents based on client briefs
- Helped map client business needs to appropriate technology solutions

#### 3.2 Market Research & Competitive Analysis
- Researched industry-relevant tools, platforms, and software solutions
- Built comparison matrices in **Microsoft Excel** to evaluate multiple options across price, features, and scalability
- Delivered research summaries to the senior consulting team for decision-making

#### 3.3 Documentation
- Drafted formal documents including project briefs, solution summaries, and process notes using **Microsoft Word** and **Google Docs**
- Maintained structured records and templates for reuse across client engagements
- Ensured all documents followed professional formatting standards

#### 3.4 Presentation Design & Support
- Created and formatted client-facing presentations using **Microsoft PowerPoint**
- Integrated Excel-based charts and data visualizations into slides
- Helped simplify complex technical information for non-technical stakeholders

#### 3.5 Data Tracking & Reporting
- Used **Microsoft Excel** and **Google Sheets** for tracking project milestones, task lists, and deliverable timelines
- Built structured tables and summary reports for internal team use
